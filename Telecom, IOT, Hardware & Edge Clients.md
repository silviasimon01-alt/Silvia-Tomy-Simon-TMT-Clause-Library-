## Drafting Recommendations & Practical Controls

**Must-have (High Risk):**
- Condition precedent for licences, type-approvals and certification before shipment/activation.
- Signed firmware & SBOM delivery; authenticated OTA update process with test restores.
- Export-control classification (ECCN) and encryption compliance schedule; deny supply to sanctioned parties.
- Detailed recall & safety incident procedure with regulatory notification timelines.

**Recommended (Medium Risk):**
- Source-code escrow for critical embedded systems with clear release triggers.
- Supplier flow-down clauses with audit rights and mandatory security clauses for OEM/ODM.
- Defined EOL & spare-part commitments, with minimum support windows for critical deployments.

**Standard (Low Risk):**
- SLAs with MTTR, monitoring telemetry access and escalation matrix.
- Routine certification renewals, annual pen-tests and ISO/TL9000 evidence.
