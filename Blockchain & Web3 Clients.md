## Drafting Recommendations & Practical Controls  

**Must-have (High Risk):**  
- **Token Legal Opinion** (classification + disclosure per MiCA/SEC).  
- **Smart Contract Audit Certificate** before deployment.  
- **AML/KYC Policy** consistent with FATF & national AML directives.  
- **DPA for On-Chain Data** covering anonymization/encryption obligations.  

**Recommended (Medium Risk):**  
- DAO governance wrapper (Wyoming DAO LLC, Swiss Verein, or Cayman Foundation).  
- Automated incident & key recovery procedure.  
- NFT resale royalty automation verification.  

**Standard (Low Risk):**  
- Standard limitation, indemnity, and arbitration clauses.  
- Force majeure clauses covering forks, 51% attacks, or regulatory bans.
