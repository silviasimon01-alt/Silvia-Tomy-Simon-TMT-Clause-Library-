# Client Categories in Technology, Media & Telecommunications (TMT)

This folder covers every major type of client a technology law team may advise.  
Each sub-file (e.g. `saas.md`, `fintech.md`) lists their typical contracts and the clauses that appear in each.

## Main Categories
1. SaaS & Cloud Service Providers
2. Platform & Marketplace Operators
3. Fintech / Insurtech / Payment Aggregators
4. Telecom / IoT / Hardware / Edge Clients
5. Creator & Media Economy Clients
6. Data & AI Platform Companies
7. Consulting / Outsourcing / BPO Clients
8. IP, M&A & JV Technology Clients
9. Cybersecurity / Reg-Tech Providers
10. Public Sector / Government-Tech / Smart-City Vendors
11. Gaming / E-Sports Clients
12. Blockchain / Web3 Clients
13. Health Tech / Med Tech Clients
