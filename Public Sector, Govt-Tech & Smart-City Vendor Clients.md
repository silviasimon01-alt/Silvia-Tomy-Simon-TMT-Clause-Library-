## 1. Government Procurement Compliance & Eligibility  

**Clause:**  
> “The Vendor represents that it is *eligible to participate in public procurement* under applicable laws, is *not debarred or blacklisted* by any government authority, and complies with all *Make-in-India and MSME participation conditions*.”

### Clause–to–Law Mapping:

| Phrase | Legal Basis | Jurisdiction | Rationale |
|--------|--------------|--------------|------------|
| “eligible to participate in public procurement” | **Rule 144(xi) GFR 2017**; **Public Procurement (Preference to Make in India) Order, 2017** | India | Eligibility conditions under GFR & DPIIT preference policy. |
| “not debarred or blacklisted” | **Rule 151 GFR 2017**; **CVC Guidelines (2022)** | India | Mandatory declaration of non-debarment. |
| “Make-in-India / MSME participation” | **PPP-MII Order 2017** (as amended 2020) | India | Ensures local value addition compliance and bid preference. |
| “public procurement eligibility” | **FAR 9.103(a)** | US | Contractor responsibility criteria under Federal Acquisition Regulation. |
| “supplier exclusion” | **Directive 2014/24/EU, Art. 57(1)** | EU | Mandatory exclusion for corruption, fraud, or collusion. |
