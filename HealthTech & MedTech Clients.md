## Drafting Recommendations & Practical Controls  

**Must-have (High Risk):**  
- Execute **Data Processing Addendum (DPA)** with detailed HIPAA/GDPR/DPDP alignment.  
- Obtain and maintain **ISO 13485** and **ISO 27001** certifications.  
- Include **regulatory classification statement** in Schedule A.  
- Maintain **audit-ready documentation** (validation, PMS, CAPA).  

**Recommended (Medium Risk):**  
- Include **Cybersecurity Annex** (encryption, patching, vulnerability testing).  
- Link **recall procedures** to indemnity and insurance clauses.  
- Create **Consent Template & Workflow** for patients/users.  

**Standard (Low Risk):**  
- Standard dispute resolution, force majeure, and termination clauses.
